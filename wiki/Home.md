# Welcome to Project Aethelgard 🏰

## 🚀 Vision: The Self-Healing Dark System

**Project Aethelgard** is a high-performance, autonomous remediation system for your MicroK8s home lab environment. By leveraging **Rust (ZeroClaw)**, **Google Jules (MCP)**, and **GitOps (FluxCD)**, we have transitioned from manual cluster management to a fully autonomous "Dark System" where the infrastructure observes and repairs itself.

### ✅ Modern Infrastructure
- **High-Performance**: Rust-native cores operational with sub-15MB RAM footprints.
- **Self-Healing**: Autonomous diagnosis and GitOps-reconciliation.
- **Enterprise Standards**: DDD, MLOps, and secure-by-design.

## 🏗️ Core Pillars

1. **Domain-Driven Design (DDD)**: Clear separation of concerns between core logic and infrastructure.
2. **MLOps Standard 2026**: Every remediation session is a tracked experiment with feedback loops.
3. **Rust Integrity**: Optimized for low footprint and high reliability on MicroK8s.
